EMG Muscle Activity Detection Circuit

Spring 2025 | MIT 6.2040 Analog Electronics Lab

Designed the signal acquisition and processing stage of a fully analog EMG (electromyography) detection system. The circuit detects small electrical signals from muscle contractions and processes them for threshold-based activation detection.

Technical Implementation:

  • Instrumentation amplifier with 1000× gain and >80dB common-mode rejection
  • Active bandpass filter (10Hz-500Hz) using Sallen-Key topology
  • Designed on single 9V supply with virtual ground reference
  • Worked through hardware debugging including virtual ground issues and filter response tuning

Tools: LTSpice for simulation, breadboard prototyping, oscilloscope characterization
